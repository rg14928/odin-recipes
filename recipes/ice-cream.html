Ice Cream

Description

The recipe will show you how to make home made vanilla ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups of heavy cream
  • 1 1/4 cups of whole milk
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon of fine sea salt
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Pour one cup of cream and add the sugar and salt to a sauce pan
  2. Warm up the mixture until the sugar dissolves then remove from the heat
  3. Add the remaining cream, milk, and vanilla extract. Whisk to combine and put in the refrigerator
  4. When ready to churn, pour into the ice cream maker. Churn according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  5. Serve immediately or transfer to a container and place it in the freezer until ready to serve
