Cheesecake

Description

This recipe will show you how to make a delicious Ney York style cheesecake. It will be a simple recipe with few ingredients.

Ingredients

Filling

  • two 8oz packs of cream cheese
  • one teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs

Crust

  • 1 1/2 cups of graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 6 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 1/8 teaspoon of salt

Instructions

Steps

  1. To make the crust: Select a pie pan and preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Combine all of the crust ingredients
  3. Press the crumbs onto the bottom and sides of the pie pan
  4. To make the filling: In a large bowl, combine the cream cheese and sugar until it is smooth
  5. Then add the eggs and vanilla, and mix until smoooth again
  6. Set the pie pan onto a baking sheet and pour the filling into the crust
  7. To bake: Bake the cheesecake for 30 minutes
  8. Remove the cheesecake from the oven and set on a rack to cool. Once cooled, it will be ready to serve.
  9. Slice cheesecake and serve with fresh fruit
